Project Overview

This independent documentary required comprehensive color grading to unify footage shot across different locations and seasons. The goal was to create a cohesive visual narrative while maintaining the authentic documentary aesthetic.

Grading Strategy

I developed a custom LUT (Look-Up Table) that established the film's visual tone while remaining flexible for individual shot adjustments. The grading process involved meticulous color correction to fix technical issues, followed by creative grading that enhanced mood and storytelling.
